No Traps seed is one thing, but ever thought that Terraria's traps are just too complex and unpredictable? Too many variations that you have to remember and watch out for?
Well, this mod aims to simplify them,
that's why it totally does not include:

- Traps painted to camouflage better, occasionally echo coated

- Vertical Dart Traps

- Venom Dart Traps replacing some normal Dart Traps

- Less obvious boulder traps

- Lava pits

- And definitely not more!


Disclaimer:
This mod only affects WorldGen, has no effect on worlds that are already generated.
Does not need to be enabled to play on worlds generated with it.
This might change in a future update.
