No Traps seed is one thing, but ever thought that Terraria's traps are just too complex and unpredictable? Too many variations that you have to remember and watch out for?
Well, this mod totally aims to simplify them,
that's why it totally does not include:

- Traps painted to camouflage better, occasionally echo coated (chance configurable)

- Less obvious boulder traps

- Weaponized Boulder Statues

- Vertical Dart Traps

- Venom Dart Traps replacing some normal Dart Traps (chance configurable)

- Ceiling geysers

- Lava pits

- And definitely not more!


Disclaimer:
This mod only affects WorldGen, has no effect on worlds that are already generated.
Does not need to be enabled to play on worlds generated with it, unless also using a mod that depends on it.

This mod is focused on reworking vanilla traps, rather than adding new ones.
